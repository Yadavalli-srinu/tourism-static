Tajmahal

Here are few lines about the Taj Mahal:
The Taj Mahal is a famous monument located in Agra, India.
It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
Construction began in 1632 and was completed around 1648, with surrounding structures finishing in 1653.
The monument is made of white marble that changes shades with sunlight and moonlight.
It is one of the finest examples of Mughal architecture, blending Persian, Islamic, and Indian styles.
The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
It is surrounded by beautiful gardens, fountains, and a reflecting pool.
The central dome is about 73 meters (240 feet) high and is flanked by four minarets.
Millions of tourists from all over the world visit the Taj Mahal each year.
It symbolizes eternal love and is often called a “teardrop on the cheek of time.”
