Tirupathi

Here are few lines about the Tirupathi:
Tirupati is a famous pilgrimage city in Andhra Pradesh, India.
It is home to the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (Balaji).
The temple is situated on the Tirumala hills, part of the Eastern Ghats.
It is one of the richest and most visited temples in the world.
Devotees offer their hair as a symbolic sacrifice to the deity.
The temple is known for its sacred laddu prasadam.
Special occasions like Brahmotsavam attract millions of pilgrims.
The city has good road, rail, and air connectivity for visitors.
Apart from the main temple, there are many other temples and holy spots nearby.
Tirupati is a spiritual hub and an important cultural landmark in India.
